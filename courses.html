Department of Information Technology and Management

The department has full-time teachers, seven demonstrators, three office assistants, one library assistant and five attendants. The department has own library with over 5000 titles. All the books in the library have been barcoded, and the issue and return of the books have been automated. There are nine class rooms with 24×7 Wi-Fi connectivity. There are several fully functional and well-equipped computer Laboratories including an electronics laboratory, having 170 systems. Apart from this, the department has multimedia projectors, printers, portable audio systems, desktops, projector screens and a generator aiding towards the effective teaching-learning process. The department has witnessed 80 per cent student placement since its inception. Some of the top recruiters are Deloitte, Cap-Gemini, Infosys, Wipro, TCS, Concentrix, Federal Bank, ICICI Bank, Tech Mahindra and Piramal Foundation. The department, at regular intervals, holds seminars, with eminent personalities from around the world as chief speakers.

Department of Information Science and Telecommunication

B.Sc.IST (Information Science & Telecommunication) is an undergraduate degree course in Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication and their applications. The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. It comprises of the Computer Sc subjects like Computer System Architecture, Operating System, Database Management System(DBMS), Oracle, Data communication & Networking, Data Structure, Software Engineering, Design and Analysis of Algorithms(DAA) ,Computer Graphics ,core programming languages like ‘C’ ,C++,and ‘java’. This course also provides students with the current state-of-the-art electronics techniques used in all modern forms of electronics communications, including Electronic Circuits, Communication Electronics, Digital Signal processing (DSP), Microprocessor & Micro Controller, Embedded System Design, Modern Communication system including optical fibre communication and mobile/cellular communication, satellite communication etc. The course gives equal emphasis to both theory and practical classes. The students also have to undergo a compulsory project work to implement the theory knowledge to a practical environment in any domain and bridge the gap between the theory and implementation in real world problem.

Department of Data Science and Management

B.Sc.DSM (Data Science and Management) is an undergraduate degree course in Computer Science, Information Technology and their Management. The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. It comprises of the Computer Sc subjects like Computer System Architecture , Digital Logics , Probability and Statistic , Organisational Behaviour, Data Structure , Alternative English , Operating System , DBMS , Decision Management System , Communicating English , core programming languages like ‘C’ , R language ,and ‘java’ . This course also provides students with the current state-of-the-art management skills . The course gives equal emphasis to both theory and practical classes. The students also have to undergo a compulsory project work to implement the theory knowledge to a practical environment in any domain and bridge the gap between the theory and implementation in real world problem. But, DSM course is permanently closed by Ravenshaw .
