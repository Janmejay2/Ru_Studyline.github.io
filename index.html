Ravenshaw Facilities

Ravenshaw University is spread over 87 acres. It offers modern amenities to students for a better learning experience. The campus is equipped with a hostel, Auditorium, Library, cafeteria, smart classrooms, Gym, Laboratory, Medical Hospital, etc.

Kanika Library

Established in 1922 and named after Sri Rajendra Narayan Bhanjadeo, Raja of Kanika, a feudatory state of Colonial Odisha, Kanika Library of Ravenshaw College/ University, is the oldest and largest library of Odisha.Kanika library play an important role in supporting education and literacy. It provide countless resources, courses, scientific publications, etc to visitors.
